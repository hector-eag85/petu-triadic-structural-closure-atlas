This file documents the formal mathematical companion to the empirical PETU atlas.

## Claim status

PETU is presented as a candidate cross-domain empirical principle and as a formal mathematical research framework.

The formal mathematical companion is theoretical and preregistration-oriented. It does not replace empirical validation and does not claim that PETU is already a completed universal law, a theory of everything, a metaphysical proof, or a theological proof.
