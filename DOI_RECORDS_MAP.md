The DOI records form a public release chain consisting of an empirical preprint, a data/code and reproducibility archive, and a formal mathematical companion.

The formal mathematical companion is theoretical and preregistration-oriented. It does not replace empirical validation and does not claim that PETU is already a completed universal law or a theory of everything.
